Summary of Results

Survey Recap

Banner appears to be unpopular among the majority of users surveyed (n=8), with 75% of initial impressions scoring a 1 or a 2.

Regarding specific categories, responses were split, with most showing a 50/50 positive-to-negative ratio. However, the advanced search feature stood out for receiving a significantly higher number of negative ratings.

The advanced search feature likely received poor reviews due to its overwhelming number of options. Filters such as "Open sections" and "Schedule Type" are unclear, while more useful options like "Course number range" are buried among less relevant ones.

Study Recap

Duration to Schedule Courses

  • Participant 1: Took 5 minutes and 42 seconds in Banner compared to 1 minute and 13 seconds in Clarify. They noted difficulty in "trying to find humanities/social science classes with open sections."
  • Participant 2: Attempted the task but gave up due to repeated system errors where Banner failed to display available classes, even when they existed.

Different Registration Systems

Participant 1:

  • Northwestern vs IIT: Northwestern's system is more condensed and simplified, but going back a tab causes issues.
  • UChicago vs IIT: Tags like closed, open, in-person, and cross-listed are better than Banner's design.

Participant 2:

  • Northwestern vs IIT: Northwestern's search tool was difficult to use, and navigating back to results reset the search page.
  • UChicago vs IIT: The search tool worked without issues.
